Observed Trends from WeatherMapy Homework

1) Temperatures are highest for equatorial cities, and decrease the further a city is from the equator; however, the graphs reflects seasonal differences in the hemispheres.
2) There is no relationship between latitude and wind speed.
3) There is a possible weak relationship between humidity and latitude, wheras cities at higher latitudes have higher humidity.
